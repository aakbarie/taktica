## Getting Started

### What is Synergy Hub?

Synergy Hub is a centralized tool for managing IT Business Relationships. It helps you:

- **Track** interactions between ITS personnel and business partners
- **Measure** satisfaction through service and work product scores
- **Analyze** trends over time
- **Manage** relationships between IT and business units
- **Resolve** issues and action items

### Main Navigation

Use the sidebar menu to navigate between different sections:

- **Home**: Welcome screen and overview
- **Dashboard**: Record new entries
- **Entries**: View all recorded interactions
- **Analysis**: See score trends and insights
- **Update**: Edit existing entries
- **Unresolved Tickets**: Manage open action items
- **Visualization**: View relationship networks
- **Manage ITS Personnel**: Add/edit personnel and relationships
- **How-To Guide**: This guide

## Recording New Entries

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to Dashboard**
   - Click "Dashboard" in the sidebar menu

2. **Enter Date**
   - Select the date of the interaction

3. **Select ITS Partner**
   - Choose the ITS staff member from the dropdown
   - This will filter the available business partners

4. **Select Business Partner**
   - Choose the business partner who was served
   - This will filter departments and divisions

5. **Select Department and Division**
   - Choose the appropriate department
   - Choose the appropriate division

6. **Enter Scores**
   - **Service Experience Score (1-10)**: Rate the service experience
     - 1 = Very Poor
     - 5 = Adequate
     - 10 = Exceptional

   - **Work Product Score (1-10)**: Rate the quality of deliverables
     - 1 = Very Poor
     - 5 = Adequate
     - 10 = Exceptional

7. **Add Comments (Optional)**
   - Provide context about the interaction
   - Describe what went well or areas for improvement
   - Note any specific challenges or successes

8. **Add Leadership Review (Optional)**
   - ITS leadership can provide oversight comments

9. **Add Action Requested (Optional)**
   - Document any follow-up actions needed

10. **Add Resolution (Optional)**
    - Document resolutions if applicable

11. **Submit**
    - Click the "Submit" button
    - A confirmation dialog will appear

### Dynamic Feedback

The system provides dynamic feedback based on your scores:

- **Low Scores (≤5)**: "Sorry to hear that! How could we improve?"
- **High Scores (≥9)**: "What do you love about the service/work product?"
- **Neutral Scores**: No additional prompt

Use these prompts to guide your comments.

## Analyzing Scores

### Analysis Tab

Navigate to the **Analysis** tab to view score trends and insights.

**What You'll See:**
- **Line Chart**: Monthly average scores (last 12 months)
  - Blue line: Average Service Experience Score
  - Beige line: Average Work Product Score

- **Markers**: Lowest scorers for each month
  - Red markers: Lowest service scores
  - Orange markers: Lowest work product scores

- **Interactive**: Hover over points for details

**How to Use:**
- Identify trends over time
- Spot problem areas (low scores)
- Celebrate successes (high scores)
- Follow up with lowest scorers
- Track improvement efforts

## Updating Entries

### Update Tab

Navigate to the **Update** tab to edit existing entries.

**Steps:**
1. Click on a row in the table to select it
2. A modal dialog will open showing:
   - **Left Panel**: Editable fields
     - ITS Leadership Review
     - Action Requested
     - Resolution
     - Completed status
   - **Right Panel**: Full comments (read-only)
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save Changes" or "Cancel"

**What You Can Edit:**
- Leadership reviews
- Action requests
- Resolutions
- Completion status

**What You Cannot Edit:**
- Date
- Personnel
- Scores
- Original comments

## Managing Unresolved Tickets

### Unresolved Tickets Tab

Navigate to the **Unresolved Tickets** tab to manage open action items.

**Features:**
- Shows only entries where Completed = "No"
- Same editing interface as Update tab
- Focus on resolution

**Steps to Resolve a Ticket:**
1. Click on an unresolved entry
2. Add resolution details
3. Change "Completed" from "No" to "Yes"
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Best Practices:**
- Review unresolved tickets weekly
- Document resolutions clearly
- Mark complete only when fully resolved
- Use this as a follow-up checklist

## Visualizing Relationships

### Visualization Tab

Navigate to the **Visualization** tab to see relationship networks.

- Shows flow from ITS Partners → Business Partners → Departments

## Managing Personnel

### Manage ITS Personnel Tab

Navigate to **Manage ITS Personnel** to manage staff and relationships.

### Managing ITS Personnel

**Add ITS Personnel:**
1. Click "Add ITS Personnel"
2. Enter the name
3. Click "Add"

**Edit ITS Personnel:**
1. Click on a cell in the table
2. Type the new value
3. Click "Save ITS Personnel"

**Delete ITS Personnel:**
1. Click on a row to select
2. Click "Delete Selected ITS Personnel"
3. Associated relationships will be removed

**Save Changes:**
- Click "Save ITS Personnel" to persist changes

### Managing Business Partners

**Add Business Partner:**
1. Click "Add Business Partner"
2. Enter name, department, and division
3. Click "Add"

**Edit Business Partner:**
1. Click on a cell in the table
2. Type the new value
3. Click "Save Business Partners"

**Delete Business Partner:**
1. Click on a row to select
2. Click "Delete Selected Business Partner"
3. Associated relationships will be removed

**Save Changes:**
- Click "Save Business Partners" to persist changes

### Managing Relationships

**Assign Business Partners to ITS Personnel:**

1. **Select ITS Personnel**
   - Use the dropdown to select an ITS staff member

2. **Assign Business Partners**
   - Check boxes for business partners to assign
   - Uncheck boxes to remove assignments

3. **Save**
   - Click "Save Relationships"
   - Confirmation dialog will appear

**Notes:**
- One ITS partner can serve multiple business partners
- One business partner can work with multiple ITS partners
- Changes are saved to the relationships file

## Tips and Best Practices

### Data Entry

✅ **Do:**
- Enter data regularly (weekly or after each interaction)
- Be honest with scores
- Provide specific comments
- Document action items clearly
- Follow up on low scores

❌ **Don't:**
- Delay data entry (details fade)
- Inflate scores
- Leave comments vague
- Ignore unresolved tickets

### Score Interpretation

- **1-3**: Critical issues, immediate attention needed
- **4-6**: Below expectations, improvement required
- **7-8**: Meets expectations, solid performance
- **9-10**: Exceeds expectations, exemplary work

### Comments

**Good Comments:**
- Specific: "Database query optimization reduced load time by 50%"
- Actionable: "Need follow-up training on new dashboard features"
- Balanced: "Great responsiveness, but documentation could be improved"

**Poor Comments:**
- Vague: "It was fine"
- Unhelpful: "Same as always"
- Incomplete: "See email"

### Workflow

**Recommended Weekly Process:**

1. **Monday**: Review unresolved tickets
2. **Throughout Week**: Record interactions
3. **Friday**:
   - Check all entries are recorded
   - Review weekly scores
   - Plan follow-ups for next week
4. **Monthly**:
   - Review analysis tab for trends
   - Adjust relationship assignments
   - Celebrate successes with team

### Data Quality

- **Consistency**: Use standard terminology
- **Completeness**: Fill in all required fields
- **Accuracy**: Double-check names and departments
- **Timeliness**: Enter data within 24-48 hours

### Privacy and Sensitivity

- Keep comments professional
- Focus on work performance, not personalities
- Use constructive language
- Protect sensitive information

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Business Partners

**Issue**: Business partner dropdown is empty

**Solution**:
1. Ensure you've selected an ITS partner first
2. Check that relationships are defined in "Manage ITS Personnel"

### Scores Not Saving

**Issue**: Entries don't appear after submitting

**Solution**:
1. Check for error messages
2. Ensure all required fields are filled
3. Verify scores are between 1-10
4. Refresh the browser

### Can't Edit Entry

**Issue**: Unable to modify an entry

**Solution**:
1. Only certain fields can be edited (Leadership Review, Action Requested, Resolution, Completed)
2. Use the Update or Unresolved Tickets tab
3. Click directly on the row to open edit dialog
